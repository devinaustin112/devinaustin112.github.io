Software Engineering, Spring 2017

Pirex was completed as a semester long project in Software Engineering, CS 345, during the fall of 2017. The program itself is a ranking information retrieval system for searching small collections of full-text documents. More specifically, the system allows the storing of books from the website www.gutenburg.org and allows the retrieval and ranking of specific passages based on search criteria specified by the user. Our group was responsible for creating the GUI as well as the backend, implementing structures such as an inverted index

Databases, Spring 2019

Name That CS Professor was completed as a semester long project in Developing Multimedia, CS 349, during the fall of 2018. The purpose of the project was to develop an interactive application that involved both visual and auditory content. Our group chose to develop a questionaire that tested student's knowledge of the computer science department at James Madison University and and allowed personalization to enhance the user's experience.
