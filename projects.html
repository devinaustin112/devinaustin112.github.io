PIREX

Software Engineering, Spring 2017

Pirex is a ranking information retrieval system for searching small collections of full-text documents. More specifically, the system allows the storing of books from the website www.gutenburg.org and allows the retrieval and ranking of specific passages based on search criteria specified by the user. Our group was responsible for creating the GUI as well as the backend, implementing structures such as an inverted index

Your Neck of the Woods

Database Design and Application, Spring 2019

Whats in Your Neck of the Woods is a three-tier application that uses a database management system. Our group chose to design an application that would allow users to look up areas of interest within certain search criteria and analyze the crime statistics of the retrieved locations. Our group was responsible for designing the SQL tables and queries as well as a front end for allowing users to interect with the data.

Name that CS Professor

Multimedia Software Development, Fall 2018

Name That CS Professor is an interactive application that utilizes both visual and auditory content. Our group chose to develop a questionaire that tested student's knowledge of the computer science department at James Madison University and and allowed personalization to enhance the user's experience.
